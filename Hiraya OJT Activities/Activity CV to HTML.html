Driver Drowsiness Detection and Notification Through Facial Pattern Analysis

A truck-mountable device that analyzes the driver’s facial pattern to determine the drowsiness level. Notification and warnings were given based on the different levels of drowsiness based on the driver’s slow closing of eyelids, yawning, and head bending. This project aims to prevent possible road-based accidents due to drowsiness and fatigue.
